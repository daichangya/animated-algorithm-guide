What is Algorithm Visualization?

Algorithm visualization transforms abstract algorithmic logic into intuitive animations. By observing how elements move, compare, and transform, you can deeply understand the core ideas behind algorithms, not just memorize code. Each algorithm has its unique "personality" - Bubble Sort's floating bubbles, Quick Sort's partitioning, Dijkstra's ripple expansion... making learning algorithms fun and engaging.
